About Us

A Taste of India,
Right Here in Princeton

Nakshatra Indian Restaurant is an authentic Indian restaurant located in the Princeton Marketplace at 4437 NJ‑27, Princeton, NJ 08540. We serve a menu inspired by both South Indian and North Indian culinary traditions, prepared with fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes.

Whether you're joining us for dine-in, picking up a takeout order, or having a meal delivered, our goal is simple: authentic flavor and warm hospitality in every dish.

  • Authentic South & North Indian recipes
  • Vegetarian options available
  • Dine-in, takeout & delivery
  • Conveniently located in Princeton Marketplace
